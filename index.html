It Is The ONLY url Contraction Service You Will EVER Need

Pass this microservice your target url as the parameter, beginning with "/new/" and then "http" or "https" and ending with the ".com" or ".org" or whatever.

What you will get back is a short code that when appended to the above url takes you to your original link. Have fun!
